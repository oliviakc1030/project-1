What Do I Actually Need To Start Baking?

I must reiterate I am no where near a pro baker. However, there are a few tools I have found to be super helpful to start baking. You may find these useful or totally useless. Nevertheles here are the tools I have collected during my baking adventures.

Baking Tools

  1. Measuring Cups & Spoons

    2. Baking is all about precision, so having a full set of measuring cups is an absolute must. I personally have a ton of different kinds of measuring cups and spoons. You do not want to be cleaning the same measuring cup or spoon over and over again in the middle of a bake. Get a few sets, you will thank yourself.

    Where To Find It?
  2. Rubber Spatula/Scraper

    3. It is important to invest in more than just one spatula unless you want to be washing one spatula over and over again. Get a bunch of spatulas in different sizes. These are useful for scraping batter from bowls and so much more.

    Where To Find It?
  3. Cake Pan

    4. If you are going to make a cake then do not try to be creative with the pan you use. You are going to want the right size of pan for the cake you are making. This will ensure an even bake and save you a headache. You can get a non-stick pan. However, always make sure to prepare your pan according to the recipie to ensure a good bake.

    Where To Find It?
  4. Rectangular & Square Baking Pan

    5. Remember cakes can come in all different shapes and sizes. These are great when making different sized cakes or tray bakes. In addition, the square baking pan is great for making brownies.

    Where To Find
  5. Pie Plate

    6. The pie plate I have linked here is the exact one I personally own. I only have one because I only bake pies about once a year. Just make sure you use the right sized pie pan for the pie you are making. Pie pans come in all different sizes. You just want to make sure you are have enough pie dough to cover the pie pan you are using.

    Where To Find It?
  6. Wire Racks

    7. I love to bake cookies and everytime I make a batch of cookies I feel like I never have enough wire racks. If you are new to baking get yourself at least two. If you love to bake cookies then get more. No one wants to wait longer then they need to for a yummy bake to cool. So get some wire racks to help simplify the problem.

    Where To Find It?
  7. Baking Sheet

    8. I would recommend purchasing at least two baking sheets at the beginning of your baking adventures. I have four and sometimes I wish I had more. I seriously do not think you can have enough but if you are new to baking just make sure you have two to start. You can always buy more later.

    Where To Find It?
  8. Rolling Pin

    9. I will admit in a pinch I have used many things to substitute a rolling pin. I may or may not have used a wine bottle. It does not work the same. Get a rolling pin. I recommend one that does not have handles.

    Where To Find It?
  9. Stand Mixer/Hand Mixer

    10. This may be someting you want to get later into your baking adventures. However, I have found this very helpful especially when baking cakes because I do not have the time to hand mix cake batter.

    Where To Find It?
  10. Parchment Paper

    11. Last but certainly not least. You will need a ton of parchment paper. It does not matter what kind you get just make sure you have some on hand all the time.
