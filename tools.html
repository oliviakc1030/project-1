What Do I Actually Need To Start Baking?

I must reiterate I am no where near a pro baker. However, there are a few tools I have found to be super helpful to start baking. You may find these useful or totally useless. Nevertheles here are the tools I have collected during my baking adventures.

My Baking Tools

  1. Measuring Cups & Spoons

    2. Baking is all about precision, so having a full set of measuring cups is an absolute must. I personally have a ton of different kinds of measuring cups and spoons. You do not want to be cleaning the same measuring cup or spoon over and over again in the middle of a bake. Get a few sets, you will thank yourself.
